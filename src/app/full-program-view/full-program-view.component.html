The Beto Paredes Family of Companies Sales Process:

We have worked diligently to make the process for sales as identical across the platforms as possible. All 6 programs follow these 12 critical steps from open to close of the deal.

1. Review the qualifications document on the Industry Report (top of the report) to make sure that you are contacting qualified leads.

2. You seek out the potential client using your own methods or prospecting (if you are granted CRM access to our leads this will be through our system).

3. Give an “Opportunity” pitch. It is critical that you cover all pitch points for the program.

4. When you have a prospect ready to go, you enter them in as a lead in our system.

5. Open the Discovery Calendar and set a Screen Share presentation with the Closing Desk and the prospect.

6. You are required to be on the Discovery presentation and host the call.

7. On the Discovery, call our senior closing team will do everything possible to move the deal towards closed, including the suggestion of sending them the contract.

8. You need to plan to stay at least 10 extra minutes after all Discoveries to speak with our Senior staff on an action plan for next steps with the prospect.

9. Once a Discovery is finished, it is your responsibility to close the rest of the contract and get the signature.

10. At this point, you send a contract request in for review to the admin team. They will add anything they see in this from your notes and then send you back the contract ready to go to the client.

11. From here, you will need to be able to answer questions on the contract the best you can and get them to sign. This is all done through digital signature (contract review is in the training center for eachopportunity).

12. Upon request, you can if needed set additional calls with the Closing Desk on a case by case approved basis.
